Punch club - a club of street fighters, in which you yourself choose the path by which you will come to your cherished goal. The club also has various rooms for relaxation and rest after long, exhausting, but useful training. We have created four schools of martial skills: the base school, the path of the bear school, the school of the tiger's path and the school of the tortoise's path. It only depends on you which style of combat and tactics you will take into service. Whether you will follow the Path of the Tiger or go the long way of the follower of the Turtle Style, or maybe you will decide to combine all possible schools. The choice is yours. But do not forget that a fighter is not only a fighting style, but also a beautiful physical form. Run, press the bars, work with the pear and, of course, do not forget to refresh yourself in time!
